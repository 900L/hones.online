üéÆ Game Mechanics

The game starts with a 4x4 grid of hidden tiles, each concealing a color. Your task is to uncover matching pairs by clicking on tiles. When you find a match, those tiles remain visible and you earn points. The game tracks your score, number of moves, and current level. As you advance through levels, new colors are introduced, making the game progressively more challenging and rewarding.
